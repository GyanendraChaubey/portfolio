Hello! I’m Gyanendra Chaubey, a tech enthusiast with a passion for pushing the boundaries of Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and GenAI. Outside of my studies and projects, I love to share my knowledge and collaborate with others, which has led me to serve in several leadership roles at IIT. I’m constantly exploring new avenues in tech, with an eye toward projects that make a tangible impact. Thanks for stopping by—I’m excited to connect and discuss anything tech-related!
